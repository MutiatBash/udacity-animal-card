Peacock

The peacock is known for its beautiful feathers and vibrant colors.

  • Scientific Name: Pavo cristatus
  • Average Length: 100-115 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 15-20 years
  • Habitat: Forests and woodlands

The peacock is a large bird species native to South Asia. Males are known for their colorful and elaborate plumage, which they use to attract females during courtship displays.
